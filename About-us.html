About us

We, (The Chernobyl Alpha Pilots), built this checklist to help students stay on top of their schoolwork—simple as that. From homework assignments to big projects, it's easy to feel overwhelmed. Our goal is to make things clearer and more manageable by breaking tasks down into checkable steps. Whether you're planning ahead or catching up, this tool is here to keep you organized, focused, and stress-free. Because getting things done feels good—and checking them off feels even better.
